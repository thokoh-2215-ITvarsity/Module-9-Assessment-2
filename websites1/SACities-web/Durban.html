Durban

Durban, a coastal city in eastern South Africa's KwaZulu-Natal province,is known for its African,Indian colonial influences. Refurbished for soccer's 2010 Wold Cup, the seafront promenade runs from uShaka Marine World, a huge theme park with an aquarium,to the futuristic Moses Mabhida Stadium. The Durban Botanical Gardens showcases African plant species.Durban is also known as the warmest place to be!Durban as a city captures the very embodinent of warmth. It also South Africa's third-largest city and known as eThekwini in Zulu langauge. Durban is known for its melting pot of cultures, beautiful coastlines, Big-5 Game experiences, friendly people,adventures,beaches and spectacular mountain rages.
